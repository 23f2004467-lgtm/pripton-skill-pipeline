## Relationship Types

1. **prerequisite** — Topic A must be learned before Topic B. Use this when the material explicitly states that B requires A, or when A is foundational to understanding B.
2. **related** — Topics are conceptually related but neither is strictly prerequisite to the other. Use for topics that belong to the same domain or are often discussed together.
3. **subtopic** — Topic A is a specific sub-concept of Topic B. Use when A is a narrower or more specific aspect of B.

## Instructions

Identify relationships between the topics above. For each relationship:
- `from_id` and `to_id` MUST be exactly one of the topic IDs listed above
- Do NOT invent topic IDs that are not in the list
- Provide a brief rationale explaining why this relationship exists

Focus on meaningful relationships. Not every topic needs to be connected. Quality over quantity.
